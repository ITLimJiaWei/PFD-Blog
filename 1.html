Lesson 1

Week 1 of Semester 4 was as expected. It was filled with module briefings and unsurprisingly almost all my classes were Home-Based. After completing all my practicals for the Week, I was planning on how I was going to approach my chosen challenge statment which is creating a system where OCBC customers can create joint accounts online. However, to my surprise I was assigned to be a team leader after filling in a padlet and a mini resume

After adding my group members to a telegram chat, lesson 2 began. During our first meeting, we came up with some questions to ask the OCBC representative, Yan Jie and researched what types of data would be required for our problem statement. We also considered non residents who do not have a SingPass account so the process would be more accommodating. After creating an XD file and google docs we concluded the meeting. In my opinion, it was quite productive and my group seems pleasant to work with. I look forward to more group meetings in the future.
