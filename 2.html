Lesson 1

Our lecturer, Joel Yang taught us about Conflict Management in groups and how different people have different ways of managing it be it confrontational or avoiding the problem. In my opinion, it is good to be confrontational but one should refrain from being hostile and be confrontational only to tackle the issue instead of insulting other team members. I learnt that most of my team and I have similar ways of managing conflict and look forward to us working together.

Team 1 and I consulted Yan Jie, the OCBC representative and we received crucial information on what OCBC wished the prototype to be. We were told not to focus too much on the business domain and rather focus on the IT implementation first. He also reminded us on the security aspects of a banking application. So far, I feel that Team 1 and I have made good progress on a wireframe and data dictionary. However, the real software developemnt begins now and we have a long way to go to a final prototype.
