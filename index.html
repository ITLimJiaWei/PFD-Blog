Week 1

Week 1 of Semester 4 was as expected. It was filled with module briefings and unsurprisingly almost all my classes were Home-Based. After completing all my practicals for the Week, I began planning on how my group and I were gonna approach our Assignment 1 in our PFD(Portfolio Development) class. Since this module is unlike previous ones, I felt that this would be an interesting challenge, especially since I was assigned Team Leader in Lesson 1.

My group and I concluded our first meeting quite productively in my opninion. We came up with some questions to ask the OCBC representative, Yan Jie and researched what types of data would be required for our problem statement which was to allow OCBC customers to create Joint accounts online. We also considered non residents who do not have a SingPass account so the process would be more accommodating.
